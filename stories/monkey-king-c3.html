The Trick of the Jade Emperor

A Throne in the Heavens or a Leash?

The Jade Emperor (玉皇大帝) sat upon his celestial throne, his gaze fixed upon the mortal world.

The Monkey King had declared himself “Great Sage Equal to Heaven (齐天大圣),” yet he had never set foot in Heaven.

"Arrogance must be guided," the Emperor mused. "If we cannot tame the beast by force, then let us tame him with illusion."

So, a royal decree descended upon Flower-Fruit Mountain (花果山).

🌿✨ Sun Wukong was “invited” to Heaven. ✨🌿

A golden chariot, drawn by celestial dragons, arrived to escort him.

The Monkey King grinned. "Ah, so they finally recognize my greatness."

But his title? Stablemaster of the Celestial Horses (弼马温).

👑 Not a lord. Not a sage. Just a caretaker of divine beasts.

At first, he enjoyed the glories of Heaven—but when he overheard the gods mocking him, his fury erupted like a volcano.

🔥 He stormed out of the stables, ripped off his robes, and returned to Flower-Fruit Mountain. 🔥

🔥 If they would not respect him—then he would take his throne by force. 🔥

The War Begins

The Army of Heaven vs. The Great Sage Equal to Heaven

Upon his return, Sun Wukong carved his title into stone:

✨ “Great Sage Equal to Heaven” (齐天大圣) – The One Above All But the Jade Emperor. ✨

The gods laughed—until the sky shook with his defiance.

🌩 A heavenly army of 100,000 was assembled, led by the Four Heavenly Kings (四大天王).

⚔️ The battle began and raged for 49 days. ⚔️

  • 🔹 Divine spears clashed against enchanted fur.
  • 🔹 Heavenly warriors fell beneath the golden staff.
  • 🔹 Even thunder and fire could not break him.

🌪 He transformed into a giant. 🔥 He multiplied into hundreds. ⚡ He laughed as gods fled.

The Jade Emperor trembled. "Call upon Erlang Shen (二郎神)."

The Duel of Legends

Sun Wukong vs. Erlang Shen

The battlefield hushed as Erlang Shen arrived.

  • 🌙 A third eye glowing.
  • ⚔️ A divine spear in hand.

Sun Wukong smiled. "Finally, a real challenge."

  • 🐉 Wukong became a dragon – Erlang a storm.
  • 🦅 Wukong a phoenix – Erlang lightning.
  • 🦢 Wukong a fish – Erlang an eagle.

But then—Wukong became a temple, hoping Erlang would not strike sacred ground.

⚡ Erlang saw through it. One blow shattered the illusion.

🔥 Laozi’s Golden Elixir Furnace (八卦炉) was opened.

🔥 Sun Wukong was thrown inside, sealed beneath roaring flames.

But he had eaten the Peaches of Immortality. He had drunk the Elixir.

🔥 The fire could not destroy him. It only made him stronger. 🔥

When the doors opened—

🌩 The Monkey King stepped out, untouched. 🌩

"Now I am truly invincible."

The Jade Emperor sent word to the Western Paradise—

“Beg Buddha for help.”
