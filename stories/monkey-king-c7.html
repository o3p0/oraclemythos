The Trial of Loyalty

"Trust, once broken, must be rebuilt in fire."

The Journey & A Hidden Threat

🐵💔 A warrior’s strength is not just in battle—but in trust. Can the Monkey King prove his devotion?

The morning air was crisp, the sun casting golden light upon the mountain path. 🌄🌿

🚶 Tang Sanzang led the way, his heart light with newfound trust in his disciple.
🐵 Sun Wukong followed, his staff resting on his shoulder.

Though the sting of the Golden Headband still lingered from their last battle, he had kept his temper in check. He had done well.

🐵 "See, Master? I can behave," he boasted, flashing a sharp-toothed grin.

🙏 Tang Sanzang chuckled.
🙏 "Perhaps, Wukong. But wisdom is not proven in one day."

🌫️ The fog thickened around them. The trees whispered as the wind carried unseen words.

🐍 A dark force slithered through the shadows.

She had been watching.
She had been waiting.

👹 The White Bone Demon (白骨精, Baigujing).

A spirit of deception. A master of illusion.
And she had one goal: To drive a wedge between the monk and his protector.

A Deadly Trick & Betrayal

👹 "A brute cannot be trusted," she mused, her lips curling into a cruel smile.
👹 "Let me show this monk what truly lurks behind the monkey’s mask."

⚡ She transformed—into a weak, human woman.
⚡ She stumbled onto the path, feigning fear.

🗣️ "Oh, kind travelers! Please, help me!" she cried, her voice trembling.

🐵 Sun Wukong’s golden eyes narrowed.
🐵 "Master, step back."

🙏 Tang Sanzang frowned.
🙏 "Wukong, she is but a woman in need."

🐵 "No, she is not."

👹 The demon-woman fell to her knees, clutching Tang Sanzang’s robes.
👹 "He lies! He is a beast, not a protector!"

🐵 Wukong’s grip tightened on his staff.
🐵 "Enough!"

⚡ With one strike—he ended her life.

🌬️ Her body vanished into the wind. Bones clattered to the ground.

👀 Tang Sanzang’s eyes widened in horror.

🙏 "Wukong… you have killed a human."

🐵 "No, Master! She was a demon!"

🙏 "I saw only a woman begging for mercy."

🐵 "You don’t understand—!"

🗣️ "Monster!" a voice called from the trees.

👹 The White Bone Demon appeared again—this time, as an old man.

👴 "He struck down my daughter without reason!"

🌿 The forest whispered with accusations.

🐵 "Lies!" Wukong snarled. "I can see through you!"

⚡ He struck again—and again, the demon vanished.

🌪️ And yet, she returned. Again. And again.

👹 The villagers’ voices rose.
🗣️ "A killer! A monster!"

🙏 Tang Sanzang’s heart ached. He turned to his disciple.

🙏 "Wukong, I can no longer trust you."

🐵 Sun Wukong’s breath caught.

🙏 "I release you from my service."

🐵 "Master—NO!"

🔥 The sutra was spoken. The Golden Headband tightened.

🐵 Pain burned through his skull.

⚡ With one final cry—Sun Wukong fled.

The Return & Redemption

🌑 The night was long.

🐵 Alone, atop a desolate peak, Sun Wukong sat in silence.

💭 Betrayed. Rejected. Cast aside.

🐵 "After all I’ve done… after all I’ve endured… he still doesn’t trust me?"

🐵 "Why did I ever try?"

💨 The wind howled through the cliffs.

🐉 A voice whispered in the night.

🗣️ "And yet, you are still watching him."

🐵 Wukong turned. A great dragon stood before him.

🐉 "If you truly did not care, would you still be here?"

🔥 Wukong clenched his fists. He hated the truth in those words.

🐵 "I swore to protect him."
🐵 "I will do so—whether he wants me or not."

⚔️ A distant scream pierced the night.

🐵 His master was in danger.

⚡ Without hesitation, Sun Wukong soared through the sky—racing toward the battle.

👹 The White Bone Demon loomed over Tang Sanzang, her fangs bared.
👹 "Now, monk… you are truly alone."

🐵 "Not while I stand!"

⚡ Sun Wukong struck with all his fury.

🔥 This time, the demon did not return.

🌿 The forest fell silent.

🙏 Tang Sanzang, trembling, looked up at his disciple.

🙏 "You came back."

🐵 "I never left."

🌅 As dawn rose, a new bond was forged.

🔥 Not through loyalty alone—but through forgiveness.
