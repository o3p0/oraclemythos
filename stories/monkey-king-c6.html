The First Demon Battle

" A protector must be more than a warrior. But can the Monkey King learn restraint?"

← →

Setup – The Journey Begins & A Dark Presence Looms

🐵⚡ A protector must be more than a warrior. But can the Monkey King learn restraint?

The road stretched before them, winding through valleys of mist and ancient trees. 🌿🏯

🚶 Tang Sanzang walked ahead, his robes flowing like water.
🐵 Sun Wukong followed, his golden eyes scanning the shadows.

For the first time in centuries, the Monkey King was free.
Yet, the weight of the Golden Headband (金箍咒) lingered on his brow.

🐵 "Master," Wukong said, swinging his staff over his shoulder. "We’ve been walking for days. When do I get to fight?"

🌿 Tang Sanzang smiled.
🙏 "Our journey is not about fighting, Wukong. It is about enlightenment."

🐵 Wukong snorted.
🐵 "Tch. Enlightenment won’t protect you from demons."

🌫️ And as if summoned by his words, the air grew heavy.
A foul wind swept through the forest. The trees shuddered.

From the shadows, a monstrous figure emerged—tall, draped in yellow robes, its face twisted with malice.

👹 The Yellow-Robed Demon (黄袍怪).
🔥 A spirit of hunger and vengeance, long forgotten by the heavens.

🗣️ "A monk walks unguarded?" the demon sneered.
🗣️ "Fortune smiles upon me. Your flesh will make a fine meal."

🔥 Sun Wukong stepped forward, his grip tightening on his staff.
🐵 "You picked the wrong monk, demon."

Conflict – A Battle of Power & Restraint

⚔️ The demon lunged!

🐵 Wukong met him in an instant—staff colliding with claws.
⚡ The earth shook beneath them. Trees splintered. The air crackled with fury.

🐵 "Is that all?" Wukong taunted, somersaulting through the air.
👹 "Arrogant beast!" the demon snarled, hurling a wave of dark fire.

🔥 Wukong spun his Golden-Banded Staff (如意金箍棒), dispersing the flames.

He grinned.
🐵 "I could end this in one blow."

And he did.

⚡ With a single strike, Wukong shattered the demon’s ribs.
⚡ The spirit collapsed, gasping.

🐵 Wukong stood over him, raising his staff for the final blow.

👹 "Mercy—!" the demon croaked.

🐵 But Wukong’s eyes burned with fury.
🐵 "Demons deserve no mercy."

He swung down.

Resolution – The Pain of the Golden Headband

⚡ Pain exploded through Wukong’s skull.

🔥 The Golden Headband tightened!
🔥 The fire of a thousand needles burned through his mind.

🐵 "AGH!" Wukong staggered, clutching his head.

🌿 Tang Sanzang spoke softly.
🙏 "Enough, Wukong."

🐵 "He is a demon!" Wukong snarled through clenched teeth.
🐵 "He would have killed you!"

🙏 "Perhaps. But if we kill without wisdom, we become no better than them."

🌿 Tang Sanzang stepped forward.

The demon, wheezing in pain, crawled away and vanished into the mist.

🐵 Wukong fumed.
🐵 "You let him escape!"

🌿 Tang Sanzang looked at him with calm eyes.
🙏 "And yet, we are still alive."

🐵 Wukong said nothing.
🐵 His fists clenched. His pride burned.

But the Golden Headband had spoken.

For the first time, his strength had been restrained.

🔥 And he hated it.

☀️ The road stretched on. The journey continued. But for Sun Wukong, the true battle had just begun.
