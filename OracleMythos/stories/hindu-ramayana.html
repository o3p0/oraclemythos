The Ramayana

Rama rescues Sita from Ravana with the help of Hanuman.

The Ramayana

Chapter 1: The Exile

Rama, along with his wife Sita and brother Lakshmana, was exiled to the forest for fourteen years, obeying his father’s vow. Their journey was filled with challenges, but their devotion and righteousness guided them through.

Chapter 2: The Abduction of Sita

Sita was abducted by Ravana, the demon king of Lanka, luring her with a golden deer. Despite her cries for help, Ravana flew her to Lanka, sparking Rama’s epic quest to rescue her.

Chapter 3: Hanuman’s Devotion

Hanuman, the monkey god, became Rama’s greatest ally. He leaped across the ocean to find Sita, delivering Rama’s message of hope and loyalty, showcasing unwavering devotion.

Chapter 4: The Battle of Lanka

Rama’s army, led by Hanuman and the Vanaras, waged a fierce battle against Ravana’s forces. Rama’s ultimate victory over Ravana symbolizes the triumph of good over evil.
