Notable Myths

The Creation of the Fifth Sun

According to Aztec mythology, the world has been destroyed and created four times, each ruled by a different sun. The current age, known as the Fifth Sun, was created by the gods after a great sacrifice.

The Journey of Huitzilopochtli

Leading the Aztec people to their homeland, Huitzilopochtli ensured their survival through his guidance and his demand for sacrifices to keep the universe in balance.

The Struggle Between Quetzalcoatl and Tezcatlipoca

In the cosmic battle between order and chaos, Quetzalcoatl and Tezcatlipoca fought for control over the heavens and the fate of mankind.
