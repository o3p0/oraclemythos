Hindu Mythology Overview

In the sacred rivers, the towering mountains, and the eternal cycle of birth, death, and rebirth, the gods of Hindu mythology guide the cosmic order. These deities, each representing different aspects of life and the universe, weave through the spiritual and material worlds.

Notable Myths

The Churning of the Ocean (Samudra Manthan)

In this epic, gods and demons churn the ocean to obtain Amrita, the nectar of immortality, with Vishnu guiding the process.

The Ramayana

Prince Rama embarks on a quest to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil.

The Bhagavad Gita

A dialogue between Krishna and Arjuna that explores the nature of duty, righteousness, and spiritual liberation.
